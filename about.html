About Me

Hello! My name is Eden Kim, and I am a senior majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Game Design and Development.

I am currently taking Accessible Computing because I believe accessibility is essential to creating meaningful and inclusive interactive experiences. Whether designing games, virtual environments, or applications, technology should be usable by as many people as possible.

Academic & Career Goals

Why Accessible Computing Matters to Me

A great game or application is built entirely around the user and how they interact with the system. Accessibility ensures that users with different abilities are not excluded from these experiences.

My long-term goal is to start my own game studio focused on immersive VR experiences. Understanding accessibility principles will help me design games that are both engaging and inclusive from the start.
